Being tall can create posture issues and back pain, which my mom has been struggling with, so she's been going to physical therapy. And my brothers might also struggle with body pains if they aren't careful while they're playing sports. It seems cool to be a physical therapist because you can help people and athletes, and I'll maybe be able to help my parents when they're older, as well as my brothers. Plus, physical therapists can make around $96,701 to $113,001. To be a physical therapist you typically need a Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree, so I plan on going to a college that provides an excellent medical education.
